Live position map

Slop discharge check
Runs the Annex I slop checker and binds the interface to either the newer unified schema or your older slop-style response shape. The UI renders special-area status, nearest-land distance, rule checklist items, and an overall permitted or not-permitted decision.
This route is specifically for slop or oil mixed water disposal logic. It highlights whether the ship is inside an Annex I special area, whether it is beyond 12 NM from nearest land, and whether the operational rule set passes.
